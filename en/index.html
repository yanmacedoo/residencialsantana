Why choose Residencial Santana?

Located in the heart of Itacaré, Residencial Santana offers the perfect combination of comfort and a prime location. Just minutes away from the main beaches and historical downtown, our accommodations are ideal for both a quick getaway and extended stays.

With fully furnished suites, lofts, and apartments, we ensure you have everything you need for a memorable stay. Equipped kitchen, air conditioning, high-speed Wi-Fi, and all the amenities to make you feel right at home.

Prime location

  • Downtown Itacaré — easy access to restaurants and shops
  • 5 minutes from the main beaches (Concha, Tiririca, Resende)
  • Close to ecological trails and tourist spots
  • Public transportation and taxis nearby
